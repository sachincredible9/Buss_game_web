Child Safety First

At Bunny Learning, we believe that education and privacy go hand in hand. All of our apps are designed with child safety as the first priority. We strictly follow COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR-K (General Data Protection Regulation for Kids) standards.

Key Principles:

  • No Tracking: We do not track users' behavior across platforms.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, addresses, or any PII (Personally Identifiable Information) from our users.
  • No Ads: We provide an ad-free environment to ensure children stay focused and safe.
