Privacy Policy for Bunny Learning

Effective Date: April 5th, 2026

1. Introduction

Bunny Learning is designed as an educational tool for young children. We understand the sensitivity of children's data and have built this app to be 100% private and child-friendly.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Our app does not require kids to create accounts, and we do not collect any personal information. Our app is compliant with COPPA (US) and GDPR-K (EU).

3. Information Collection

We collect zero personally identifiable information. We do not track location, use device IDs for marketing, or request any sensitive permissions like access to the camera or contacts.

4. No Third-Party Advertising

There are no third-party advertisements in Bunny Learning. We believe ads can be distracting and potentially unsafe for children.

5. External Links & Parental Gate

Any links to the App Store or our website are protected by a "Parental Gate" to prevent children from leaving the app without adult supervision.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out to us at support-bunnylearning@gmail.com.
